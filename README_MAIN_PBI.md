### **UK Broadband Performance Dashboard**

![Tool](https://img.shields.io/badge/Tool-Power%20BI-F2C811?style=flat)
![Domain](https://img.shields.io/badge/Domain-Telecommunications-lightgrey?style=flat)

![Source](https://img.shields.io/badge/Source-Ofcom-lightgrey?style=flat)
![Dataset](https://img.shields.io/badge/Dataset-Ofcom-blue?style=flat)

**Focus**
Speed, pricing efficiency, coverage, and provider benchmarking across the UK.

**Dataset**

* **Source**: Ofcom
* **Dataset**: UK Broadband Open Data
* **Link**: [UK Broadband Open Data](https://www.ofcom.org.uk/about-ofcom/our-research/opendata)

**Business Questions Answered**

* How does **broadband pricing** compare across different regions and providers?
* What are the **performance gaps** between rural and urban broadband?
* Which providers are **underperforming** compared to others in the same price range?

**Key Insights Generated**

* Revealed a significant **urban vs rural performance gap** in broadband speeds.
* Identified **underperforming premium providers** with high pricing but low speeds.
* Delivered actionable insights into **provider competition** and **cost per Mbps**.

## **Datasets & Sources**

All dashboards in this repository are built using **publicly available datasets** from reputable platforms such as **Kaggle**, **Ofcom**, and other open data providers. This ensures transparency, reproducibility, and compliance with data privacy standards.

### Datasets used in the dashboards:

* **Video Games**: [Video Game Sales with Ratings](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rush4ratio/video-game-sales-with-ratings)
* **UK Rail**: [National Rail UK Train Ticket Data](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/motsimaslam/national-rail-uk-train-ticket-data)
* **LEGO**: [LEGO Sets & Themes Database](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jkraak/lego-sets-and-themes-database)
* **Broadband**: [UK Broadband Open Data](https://www.ofcom.org.uk/about-ofcom/our-research/opendata)
* **Airlines**: [Airline Passenger Satisfaction](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/teejmahal20/airline-passenger-satisfaction)
* **Loans**: [Loan Status Prediction Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bhavikjikadara/loan-status-prediction)
* **Retail Sales**: [Superstore Sales Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohitsahoo/sales-forecasting)
