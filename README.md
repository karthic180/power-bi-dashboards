## **Business Questions Answered**

- Which stations generate the highest passenger volume?  
- What are the main causes of journey delays?  
- How do ticket types and classes contribute to revenue?  
- What drives refund requests, and how can they be reduced?  
- How do customer purchase behaviours vary across channels and payment methods?  

---

## **Key Insights Summary**

- **London King’s Cross** is the busiest departure station, exceeding 50,000 journeys.  
- **Signal failures** are the leading cause of delays and refund claims.  
- **Online purchases (58%)** dominate, with credit cards as the most common payment method.  
- **Revenue exceeds £30M**, driven by Anytime and Return tickets.  
- **Average ticket price: £23.44**, with First Class averaging £75.  
- Only **3% of journeys** result in refunds, mostly due to delays over 30 minutes.  
- **Standard Class = 90%** of journeys, though First Class contributes disproportionately to revenue.
