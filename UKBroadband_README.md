

#  **README – UK Broadband Performance Dashboard**

##  **Overview**
This dashboard provides a structured view of UK broadband performance using **mock data** designed to resemble real Ofcom broadband reporting. It highlights how broadband providers and UK regions compare across key performance indicators such as download speed, upload speed, rural vs. urban performance, and average monthly cost.

The dashboard is intended for demonstration, analysis practice, and BI portfolio use.

---

##  **Data Structure**
Although the dashboard uses mock data, it mirrors the fields commonly found in UK broadband datasets:

### **Provider-Level Metrics**
- Average download speed  
- Average upload speed  
- Average monthly cost  
- Speed per £ (value metric)  
- Provider ranking  

### **Region-Level Metrics**
- Average download speed  
- Average monthly cost  
- Rural vs. urban speed comparison  
- Rural coverage percentage  

### **Global KPIs**
- Avg Urban Speed  
- Avg Rural Speed  
- Avg Monthly Cost  
- Rural %  
- Top UK Provider  
- Top UK Region  

---

##  **Dashboard Pages**

### **1. Overview**
A high-level summary of broadband performance across the UK, including:
- Top provider

### **2. Providers**
Comparisons across major UK ISPs:
- BT  
- Sky

- Vodafone

- Plusnet  

Visuals include:
- Avg monthly cost  
- Avg download speed

### **3. Regions**
Regional broadband performance across:
- East Midlands  
- London  
- North West  
- Northern Ireland  
- Scotland  
- South East  
- South West  
- Wales  
- West Midlands  

Visuals include:
- Avg cost by region  
- Avg download speed  
- Rural vs. urban speed  

### **4. Cost & Speed Analysis**
Combined charts showing:
- Monthly cost vs. download speed  
- Upload vs. download performance  
- Provider and region cross‑comparison
